How to Enable Unknown Sources on Android
  1. Open Settings on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to Security or Apps & notifications.
  3. Find and tap Install unknown apps.
  4. Select the app (e.g., your browser or file manager).
  5. Toggle on Allow from this source.
