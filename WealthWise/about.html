About WealthWise

This application is meant to be an application that helps it's users realize their spending trends. It will take in transactions and see the category it's meant to be in (e.g. fast food which is part of food) and see if there's better alternatives.
Due to the scope of how sensitive and difficult it is when it comes to dealing with financial data and banks, a dummy bank was made to account for 'transactions' (Thank you Tyler).
The application has dark and light theme, and easy to navigate.
